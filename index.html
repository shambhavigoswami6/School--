Hi!

Frank J. Dugan Elementary School opened to kindergarten through sixth-grade students and staff in January 1987 with Mr. Thomas Ellsworth as principal. The school is located in the southern end of Marlboro Township on Topanemus Road. Presently, we serve approximately 600 students in kindergarten through fifth grade. Our current building administrators are Principal Richard Pagliaro and Vice Principal Sashi Gundala who oversee more than 60 faculty and staff.
